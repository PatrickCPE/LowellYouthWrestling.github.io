About Us

This site is home for Lowell Youth Wrestling out of Lowell MA

Practice Details

Practices are held twice per week, on Monday and Friday from 6:00-7:30 PM

Warm-ups start at 5:45 PM. If you're not early, you're late!

Occasionally Saturday practices will be held, and will be announced via Remind.

Practices are held at Doughboy Wrestling Club: 171 Lincoln St, Lowell, MA 01852, USA

For the Wrestlers

  1. All wrestlers must take off their outside shoes before walking on the mats!
  2. Access the locker rooms through the wrestling rooms, not via the weight room!
  3. Wrestlers should put all belongings in a locker before practice, or find a spot on the floor in the locker room.
